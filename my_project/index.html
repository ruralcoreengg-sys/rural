Our Story

Serving Industry With Ruralcore Since 2026

Founded in 2026 in Chennai, Ruralcore CAD Solutions started with a vision to provide high-quality CAD drafting and engineering documentation services for the manufacturing and fabrication industry.

From supporting local pressure vessel manufacturers to serving fabricators, EPC contractors, and process equipment companies, Ruralcore has rapidly grown into a trusted engineering solutions partner.

We specialize in delivering precise, industry-standard, and production-ready drawings with a strong focus on accuracy, quality, and on-time delivery. Our commitment to technical excellence and client satisfaction helps industries streamline design, fabrication, and documentation processes efficiently.

What We Offer

Engineering Services

Comprehensive CAD drafting and engineering documentation for fabrication and industrial sectors.

Pressure Vessel Redrawing

We redraw existing pressure vessel GA drawings using updated dimensions and applicable standards.

  • ASME Sec VIII & IS 2825
  • Updated nozzle schedules
  • DWG, PDF, DXF formats
  • 24–72 hour turnaround

AutoCAD 2D Drafting

Precision 2D drafting for GA, fabrication, assembly drawings from sketches or PDFs.

  • ISO 128 / ISO 10209
  • Multi-view projections
  • Clean layer structures
  • Revision tracking

Nozzle Orientation

Detailed nozzle orientation drawings for vessels, columns and heat exchangers.

  • Plan & elevation views
  • ANSI/ASME flanges
  • P&ID coordination
  • Coordinate dimensioning

3D Modeling

Parametric 3D models in SolidWorks or Fusion 360 for custom components.

  • Interference check
  • Photorealistic renders
  • STEP / IGES / STL
  • Design for manufacturing

BOM Preparation

Structured BOM from assemblies or 3D models with material specs.

  • Multi-level BOM
  • Excel / CSV outputs
  • ERP/SAP-ready
  • Title block integration

Drawing Revision

Incorporating red-line markups and site modifications into CAD drawings.

  • Revision cloud marking
  • As-built preparation
  • Full audit trail
  • PDF + DWG deliverables
